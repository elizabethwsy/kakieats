A food tour is a guided walking tour by one of our Kaki guides where you will taste samples at local street stalls and restaurants serving Singapore food. In addition, you will learn about the history and culture of Singapore as you walk in the neighbourhoods.

Our food tours usually last about 4 hours.

Most of our tours are walking tours so during much of the 4 hours, our customers will be standing or walking. The total distance covered is approximately 2 km. We will have seating arrangements provided at some of the tasting locations. There will also be at least 2 restroom breaks during the tour.

No. The tour ends at a different location from the starting point. Detailed directions to both the starting and ending points will be provided with the confirmation email after ticket purchase. All participants will receive a map with the tour route and directions to the closest MRT stations. Our Kaki guides would be more than happy to give you the directions after the tour.

Tours are held rain or shine. We also provide ponchos in case of sudden weather changes and you do not have your own umbrella. We only cancel our tour in an unlikely event of extreme bad weather. In that case, we will try our best to reschedule your tour or refund.

Tours are only offered primarily in English at this time. We offer tours in mandarin as well but prior arrangements have to be made.

We offer vegetarian and pescetarian tastings on all Kaki Tours! All of our food and drink tastings are arranged in advance, so we ask that you note this in your online ticket purchase.

Unfortunately, we are unable to guarantee substitutions for other dietary restrictions such as: Gluten-Free, Dairy-Free, Vegan, and other food allergies. For these diets, the Little India Tour is your best option, but you may miss out on a number of tastings. The Michelin and Supper Tours are not recommended for these dietary restrictions and we will contact you to refund your purchase.

If you leave us a note in your online ticket purchase, we will order alternative tastings wherever possible. In all cases, our Kaki guides will inform the group of the ingredients in each dish and leave it to the final discretion of each guest to partake.

Please note, we cannot guarantee that cross-contamination may not occur due to the nature of our tasting locations. If you have severe food allergies, please contact us at hellokakieats@gmail.com or 6777 8311 before purchasing tickets.

Yes! We offer vegetarian and pescetarian tastings on all Kaki Tours! All of our food and drink tastings are arranged in advance, so we ask that you note this in your online ticket purchase.

However, we apologise that none of our Kaki Tours are 100% gluten-free. The Chinatown Tour has the fewest tastings that contain gluten and has been reviewed as a great experience by other gluten-friendly guests. The Supper Tour and Little India Tour are not recommended gluten-free diets and we will contact you to refund your purchase.

The Amazing Race is designed to be a fun event for the whole family. For children who have a limited attention span, this tour may not be appropriate for them. Parents, who know their children best, should assess the situation individually.

Children 6 years old and under will not be eating do not require a ticket. However, please email us in advance to let us know that you’ll have a little one with you! Tickets are required for children over 6, regardless of whether they will be participating in tastings.

For the most part, the Chinatown Tour, Kampong Glam and Little India Tour can be catered to accommodate wheelchairs and baby strollers. If you have a mobility issue or plan on using a wheelchair or stroller and it prevents you from going up stairs, please email us after you complete your booking so that we are able to make special arrangements for you.

Sure! We welcome everyone, pregnant or not. Please note that there will be a bit of walking and standing but we have seating arrangements available at some of our tasting locations. We will also do our best to accommodate your pace.

In addition to a big appetite, please bring along your confirmation email. In terms of clothing, we suggest you wear comfortable clothing and shoes. Singapore can be very hot, it is recommended that you wear sun protection, including sun block, sunglasses or a hat. We will provide a bottle of water for you at the first tasting location. There will also be drinks provided at some of our other tasting locations.

No. Gratuities are not included in the ticket price. If you think our Kaki guides have done a great job, they would greatly appreciate any gratuities.

Yes, there is paid public parkades located near the meeting point of the Chinatown Tour and Little India Tour. Please do not park in metered parking spots as most have a limit of only two hours. There are free 3-hour parking spots at select locations within Chinatown.

We encourage our participants to shop after the tour. To ensure that you have the best tasting experience, we have worked with each of our eateries to provide freshly-made food and drinks. It is important that we stay on schedule and arrive on time at each of our six locations. We will provide a detailed map showing our route so that you could return to each of the tasting location at your leisure.

Sorry…due to the various food establishments we visit, pets are not allowed on the tour.
