Chinatown Tour

Tour Details

Savour a variety of sumptuous food and drinks as our guide share how these cuisines is central to Singaporeans’ lifestyles. Experience for yourself the transformation of Chinatown from its early colonial days to modern day Singapore through your tastebuds. .

Time: 10 am
Duration: 4 hours
Meeting point: Chinatown MRT
Ground Level Exit A (Escalator Side)
View Map
Tour mode: On Foot
Cost: Solo yolo: $70 per person   Group: $60 per person

Itinerary

Chinatown Complex

Buddha Tooth Relic Temple

Banda Street

URA’s City Gallery

Maxwell Hawker Centre

What to Expect

6–7 authentic tasting locations
9-10 delicious samples which include Hainanese Chicken Rice, Kaya Toast, Carrot Cake, Char Kway Teow and many more
Experienced, friendly and entertaining kaki guide
Fun & informative guided walking tour

Things to Note

Confirmation ticket email
Big appetite and huge smiles
Total walking distance is about 2km on flat grounds (with lots of breaks in between) but there may be some staircases during journey
Light tropical clothing is recommended with comfortable walking shoes
