Kaki Eats is back with our Countdown Party

The long awaited countdown party is back! Meet new kakis or reunite with other kakis at our annual countdown party for a night of good ol’ fun, great food and even better company. Usher in the new year with Kaki Eats and Hopheads Bar as we bring you and your kakis an experience like never before!

Hopheads, is an eclectic mish-mash of things to do, eat and most importantly, drink. The folks behind Hopheads keep things fresh by rotating the craft beer they have on tap regularly, switching out a particular brew for another when its barrel runs dry. And our countdown party ensures that you have a free flow of craft beer throughout the night. What better way to toast to new beginnings than sharing beer with your friends?

For those hungry souls out there, there will also be a free flow of Hopshead Bar signature salted egg yolk pasta, which features fried breaded prawns atop spaghetti in a creamy, salted egg yolk sauce. A local spin on carbonara, this dish had a noteworthy take on local flavours, and the generous portion makes it great for sharing. Not in the mood for pasta? Alternatives include burgers and snacks such as the chilli beef nachos and patata bravas.

So what sets our countdown event apart from others out there? Besides free flow of delectable craft beers and fusion food, our event has been outfitted to keep you and your kaki entertained till last call. Put on your dancing shoes and party the night away as we feature DJ SoulRocka in the house. There is also a huge collection of party-worthy games like beer pong and darts at your disposal.

So while the rest of the F&B world jostles for your attention with newfangled dishes and “exclusive” and premium drink menus, you can count on Kaki Eats for some good ol’ no frills fun this new year.

Date: 31 December 2018, Monday
Time: 10PM onwards
Venue: Hopheads Bar, #B1, 178 Clemenceau Ave, 00 Haw Par Glass Tower, 239926
Price: $80 per pax
10% discount on the next ticket if you grab a kaki along
