About Us

Kaki is a local slang for buddies, and in Singapore, eating is our favourite national past time. So what better way to savour the best of local eats than enjoy it with a group of friends!

At Kaki Eats, we believe that food should be enjoyed with a group of friends. Whether it is dining with your new or old friends, we want you to experience the best of local flavours in the comfort of a friendly group setting.

With our cosy groups, you will come as a visitor and leave as a kaki.
